Public Enemy Number One

During the 1930s Depression, many Americans, nearly helpless against forces they didnâ€™t understand, made heroes of outlaws who took what they wanted at gunpoint. Of all the lurid desperadoes, one man, John Herbert Dillinger, came to evoke this Gangster Era and stirred mass emotion to a degree rarely seen in this country.

Dillinger, whose name once dominated the headlines, was a notorious and vicious thief. From September 1933 until July 1934, he and his violent gang terrorized the Midwest, robbing banks and police arsenals, and staging 3 jail breaks.

Here at Dillinger Distillery, we attempt to carefully re-create that era, both it's beauty and ugly. We use the elements of our local history to push our bright future. Come sip on one of our finely crafted spirits today, and experience history in your own perspective.
